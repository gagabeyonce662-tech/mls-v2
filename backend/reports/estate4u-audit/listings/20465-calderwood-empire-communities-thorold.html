<h3>Discover the Luxurious Living at Empire Calderwood in Thorold</h3>
Empire Calderwood by Empire Communities is a new townhome development situated at Davis Rd &amp; Barker Pkwy in Thorold. It offers spacious towns and detached homes in close proximity to the world-famous Niagara Falls and its renowned wineries and vineyards. Residents will enjoy convenient access to lifestyle amenities, commuting options, and top-rated schools such as Brock University and Niagara College. Additionally, major highways, the Pen Centre, and St. Catharines are just a stone's throw away, providing luxury and convenience at Calderwood.
